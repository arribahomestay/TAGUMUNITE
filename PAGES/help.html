Help & guide

Short tips for using Tagum UYnite as an admin.

Issues

View and manage resident reports coming from Tagum City.

  • Use filters to find reports by barangay, status, or category.
  • Open a report to see photos, location, and conversation.
  • Coordinate with departments via Messages before scheduling work.

Maps

Locate reports and areas of concern around Tagum City.

  • Search using barangay names or street names only within Tagum.
  • Combine map view with Issues list to prioritize areas.

Messages

Coordinate work with departments and barangay coordinators.

  • Select a department channel (e.g. City Engineering, Traffic Management).
  • Share report IDs, schedules, and status updates.
  • Use Messages before updating a report to keep everyone aligned.

Schedules

Plan field actions for reports across Tagum City.

  • Use the weekly view to balance workload between barangays.
  • Keep unscheduled high-priority reports to a minimum.
  • Record who is assigned and any notes for coordination.

Need more help?

For questions about Tagum UYnite workflows, contact your system administrator or LGU IT support team.

You can also document your own internal processes here later (who approves which reports, escalation flow, etc.).
